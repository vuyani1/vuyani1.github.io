WHAT
SQUIDS DOES

Every feature exists to make the conversation better, not the metrics.

Communities

Topic-based spaces where people who care about the same things can find each other and talk.

▲

Ranked Feed

The best posts surface through upvotes and engagement. Algorithmic, but transparent.

💬

Threaded Comments

Nested replies that stay organised no matter how deep the conversation goes.

🔔

Notifications

Real-time alerts for replies, upvotes, and community activity.

A clear modal warns you before navigating to any external site. No silent redirects.

👤

Profiles

Post history, community activity, and a public profile — with privacy controls that make sense.

Flexible Auth

Sign in with Google or email. Both work. Both are secure.

📸

Media Uploads

Avatar and community banner image support with fast Cloudflare-powered delivery.
