About

Built with purpose.
Grounded in truth.

SQUIDS is more than a platform. It's a space built with purpose — a place where people come not just to consume ideas, but to shape them, challenge them, and bring better ones to life.

Our slogan, "Where ideas surface," isn't about passively finding inspiration. It's about something deeper. What you see, read, and experience here should push you to think further, question more, and refine your own vision.

SQUIDS was created with a clear mission: to build a space where truth matters, where young people are informed, and where real conversations shape the future.

We are especially focused on creating a platform that represents Africa as it truly is — its growth, its challenges, its innovation, and its people. For too long, conversations about the continent have been shaped elsewhere.

Here, we tell our own stories. We share our own knowledge. We define our own narrative.

Mission

A different kind of platform.

SQUIDS takes a clear stand: this is a space for human voices, lived experiences, and authenticity. The next generation deserves clarity — not confusion.

Africa's Narrative

For too long, conversations about Africa have been shaped elsewhere. Here, Africans tell their own stories, share their own knowledge, and define their own narrative.

Human Voices Only

We are actively working to limit the influence of AI-generated content and bots. This is a space for real thoughts and real experiences.

Youth First

Young people deserve accurate, empowering information and a community they can trust. We build with them in mind — always.

Growing Across Africa

SQUIDS is growing into a platform that reaches across the continent — connecting people, ideas, and opportunities.

Why "Squids"?

Squids have three hearts.

At SQUIDS, that means something to us. We believe everyone deserves more than one chance — in fact, we believe in three. But just like those three hearts, they can all be broken at once if we forget what matters. So we ask for one simple thing: Respect.
