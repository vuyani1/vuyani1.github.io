Tyvila is an online African entrepreneur and intrapreneur company building a safe space for startups, growing businesses, creators, and builders. About Tyvila

Founded by Vuyani Siyanda Vilakazi, a young Black African entrepreneur, Tyvila exists to inspire, support, and elevate African innovation. Our mission is to help entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs build sustainable digital products, businesses, and platforms that scale globally while remaining rooted in African culture and growth.
